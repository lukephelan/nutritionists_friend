app_user_type

Each row identifies a user type, which will be either “admin” or “client”. This will affect what sort of permissions they have over the database and what their dashboard is like. For example, “client” can only view data that matches their own ID. In contrast, “admin” can view the information of all users.

  • app_user_type_id:
  • app_user_type_description:text
